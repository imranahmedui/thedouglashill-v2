About the Author

Douglas Hill is a passionate home inspector with over a decade of experience in the South Florida real estate market. His commitment to providing exceptional service to home buyers led to the creation of Ground Up South Home Inspections. Established in 2014 in the South Florida region, his company plans to continue to provide home inspections for the real estate industry, home buyers, brokers, appraisals, and insurance agents.

This company was started by him as a result of his belief regarding the need to provide pertinent information to home buyers in terms of the inspection aspect.
